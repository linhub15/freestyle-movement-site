Saturday June 15, 2019

6:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Join us as we celebrate Edmonton's Hiphop community dancers. We are bringing together universities, high schools, dance groups and crews to showcase their talent and hard work.

Ticket proceeds will help Freestyle Movement build up the community by hosting workhops, events and providing a space for dancers to practice.
