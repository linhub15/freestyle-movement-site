Brookview Breaking Class 2022

Thank you for your interest in Freestyle Movement Breaking Program. The goal for this program is to introduce dancers to a unique set of foundation movements in Breaking. These movements will be taught step by step and chained together to form combos and sets.

As the dancer progresses, they will experience freestyling in a cypher circle where each dancer takes a turn expressing their interpretation of the music with the moves they have practiced. A cypher is where each dancer exchanges with the other dancers.

For those that feel comfortable, opportunities to compete in Breaking battles will come up throughout the year. Battles have been recently added as an olympic sport and is judged unlike cyphering. Battling uses the same movements in Breaking but the purpose is to one-up (out do) your opponent through musicality, difficulty, and creativity all bundled together with overall execution.

As a dancer you will definitely have fun learning breaking and get some good exercise while you're at it!
